Simple Pancake Recipe

This easy pancake recipe is perfect for a quick breakfast. It uses simple ingredients and takes only a few minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and egg in a bowl.
  2. Slowly add the milk and stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour some batter.
  4. Cook until golden brown on both sides and serve.
